Immutable

Although you can make as many prediction changes as you want, prediction history is never deleted.

Your Information, Undamaged

BallerBlok's use of the EVM basically assures there is no risk of prediction tampering or information mishandling

Your Predictions Belong To You

Each user's address maintains up to one pick from each category at all times, unable to be changed by anyone but the user.


You can use the search feature to verify that your predictions have been recorded accurately, or to give to others to tip for accurate predictions.

Collect Funds

Just send somebody your address or username and your information will appear alongside your predictions. This lets anybody tip you for your predictions if they choose!


Get Started Now:

  1. Make sure you're in a Web3 enabled browser like Chrome with MetaMask, Brave, or Mist.
  2. Choose from the NCAA/NBA options listed above or follow link.
  3. Choose your team predictions.
  4. Save your predictions to the Ethereum blockchain by clicking "Submit".
  5. When the transaction is confirmed, go to snipbin.co/search/[your address or username] and check out your prediction!
